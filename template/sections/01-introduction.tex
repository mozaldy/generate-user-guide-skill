\section{Introduction}

\subsection{Purpose}
This guide provides comprehensive instructions for using
\textbf{Insight Doc} (also referred to as \textbf{SGN Insight}), an
enterprise knowledge management platform developed by
PT.\ Sinergi Dimensi Informatika (Sindika). It is designed to
transform scattered, uncontrolled documents --- SOPs, policies, manuals,
technical records, and strategic assets --- into a structured,
secure, and intelligent organizational knowledge base.

This document covers every aspect of daily operation, from first-time
login and basic navigation through advanced workflows such as
document approval chains, AI-powered search, secure download with
rasterized watermarking, and role-based analytics dashboards.

\subsection{The Problem Insight Doc Solves}

Organizations frequently struggle with fragmented, uncontrolled, and
vulnerable knowledge assets. Insight Doc was built to address the
following pain points:

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{} l X @{}}
  \toprule
  \textbf{Problem} & \textbf{Description} \\
  \midrule
  Scattered Documents
    & Critical SOPs, policies, and manuals spread across shared
      drives, emails, and physical files \\
  Uncontrolled Sharing
    & Sensitive documents shared without access controls, increasing
      data leakage risk \\
  Version Confusion
    & Multiple versions circulate with no tracking --- employees may
      follow obsolete procedures \\
  No Audit Trail
    & No systematic tracking of who accessed, modified, or shared
      critical documents \\
  Data Leakage Risk
    & Intellectual property and strategic records vulnerable without
      systematic access control \\
  Weak Governance
    & No structured lifecycle management for document expiry, review,
      and compliance \\
  \bottomrule
\end{tabularx}

\subsection{Scope}
This document covers the following functional areas of the platform:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=18pt]
  \item System access, SSO authentication, and role assignment
  \item Document management --- upload, metadata, categorization,
        and full-text search
  \item Version control --- immutable revision history and file
        integrity (SHA-256)
  \item Approval workflows --- six-status lifecycle with audit trail
  \item Document preview --- in-browser viewing with HTTP Range
        streaming
  \item Secure download --- PDF rasterization, embedded watermarking,
        and rate limiting
  \item Share links and download tokens --- public sharing with
        expiry, download limits, and revocation
  \item Access control --- per-document ACL with user/role grants
        and time-limited access
  \item Access request workflow --- self-service request, approval,
        and automatic provisioning
  \item Audit, analytics, and unified activity timeline
  \item Role-based dashboards --- Admin/Manager, Reviewer, and Staff
  \item AI-powered intelligence --- full-text + semantic search and
        RAG-based chat assistant
  \item Master data administration --- document types, statuses,
        organizational units, and ACL groups
  \item Lifecycle management --- soft delete, restore, and
        auto-archive on expiry
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Prerequisites}

\subsubsection*{For End Users}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=18pt]
  \item Active corporate account provisioned via \textbf{Keycloak SSO}
        with an assigned role
  \item Modern web browser (Chrome 120+, Firefox 115+, Edge 120+)
  \item Stable internet connection (minimum 5~Mbps recommended)
  \item Basic familiarity with web-based business applications
\end{itemize}

\subsubsection*{For Administrators}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=18pt]
  \item All end-user prerequisites above
  \item Access to the \textbf{Keycloak Administration Console} for
        user and role management
  \item Familiarity with organizational unit structure and document
        classification policies
\end{itemize}

\begin{ugNote}
  If you do not have an account, contact the IT Help Desk at
  \texttt{helpdesk@sindika.co.id} or extension \textbf{4500}.
  Account provisioning is managed centrally through the Keycloak
  identity provider.
\end{ugNote}

\subsection{How to Use This Guide}

This guide is organized in a progressive structure, starting
with high-level concepts and moving toward detailed task
instructions:

\begin{ugSteps}
  \item \textbf{Sections 2 -- 3} provide system context ---
    read these first to understand what Insight Doc does and
    how it is architected.
  \item \textbf{Section 4} walks you through first-time login
    and initial setup --- essential for new users.
  \item \textbf{Section 5} gives a visual tour of the interface
    --- review this to familiarize yourself with navigation.
  \item \textbf{Section 6} describes every functional module in
    detail --- use this as a reference when exploring features.
  \item \textbf{Section 7} provides step-by-step task guides
    for common day-to-day activities.
  \item \textbf{Sections 8 -- 9} cover troubleshooting and FAQ
    --- consult these when you encounter issues.
  \item \textbf{Sections 10 -- 12} contain best practices, the
    glossary, and appendices --- reference materials for
    advanced usage.
\end{ugSteps}
