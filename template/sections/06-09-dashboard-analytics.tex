\section{Dashboard Analytics}

\begin{ugModule}{Overview}

  \textbf{Purpose:}
  Provide role-specific dashboards with real-time insights and
  actionable metrics.

  \textbf{Who Can Access:}
  All authenticated users (dashboard content varies by role).
\end{ugModule}

\subsection{Admin / Manager Dashboard}

The administrative dashboard provides a full organizational
overview:

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{} l X @{}}
  \toprule
  \textbf{Widget} & \textbf{Details} \\
  \midrule
  Overview Cards
    & Total documents, active, archived, deleted, total
      versions, storage usage, total downloads,
      unique users \\
  Download Trend
    & 30-day area chart showing daily download volume \\
  Status Distribution
    & Doughnut chart showing document count per status \\
  Type Distribution
    & Horizontal bar chart by document type
      (SOP, SK, etc.) \\
  Unit Distribution
    & Horizontal bar chart by organizational unit \\
  Top~10 Documents
    & Most downloaded documents (title, type,
      download count) \\
  Pending Items
    & Alert cards: review pending, access requests
      pending, documents expiring within 7~days,
      access grants expiring within 7~days \\
  Recent Activity
    & Live feed of the 20 most recent system
      activities \\
  \bottomrule
\end{tabularx}

\subsection{Reviewer Dashboard}

The reviewer dashboard focuses on the approval pipeline:

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{} l X @{}}
  \toprule
  \textbf{Widget} & \textbf{Details} \\
  \midrule
  Review Pipeline
    & Stat cards: pending, approved (this month),
      rejected (this month) \\
  Status Pipeline
    & Stacked bar visualization of all documents
      per status \\
  Pending Review Table
    & Documents awaiting review, sorted oldest-first
      (urgency priority) \\
  Recent Status Changes
    & Timeline of the 20 most recent status
      transitions \\
  \bottomrule
\end{tabularx}

\subsection{Staff Dashboard}

The staff dashboard shows personal document activity:

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{} l X @{}}
  \toprule
  \textbf{Widget} & \textbf{Details} \\
  \midrule
  My Documents
    & Stat cards: total owned, in review,
      rejected \\
  Status Donut
    & Mini donut chart of your documents
      by status \\
  Draft Quick Actions
    & Card list of your draft documents with
      Edit and Submit buttons \\
  My Access Requests
    & Status of your pending, approved, and
      rejected access requests \\
  My Activity Feed
    & Personal timeline of your 15 most recent
      actions \\
  \bottomrule
\end{tabularx}

\subsection{Dashboard Refresh \& Real-Time Updates}

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=18pt]
  \item Dashboard data is loaded on page visit and refreshed
        via API calls when navigating back to the dashboard.
  \item Real-time notification badges (pending review count,
        new access requests) update via WebSocket connections.
  \item Charts use responsive sizing and adapt to the browser
        window width.
\end{itemize}

\begin{ugTip}
  Check your dashboard at the start of each work day to stay
  on top of pending review requests, access requests, and
  documents nearing expiry.
\end{ugTip}
