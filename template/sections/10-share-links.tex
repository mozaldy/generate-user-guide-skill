\section{Share Links \& Download Tokens}

\begin{ugModule}{Overview}

  \textbf{Purpose:}
  Share documents with external recipients who do not have
  system accounts, using secure, controlled links.

  \textbf{Who Can Access:}
  \ugRole{Admin} \ugRole{Manager}
\end{ugModule}

\subsection{Share Links vs.\ Download Tokens}

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{} l X X @{}}
  \toprule
  \textbf{Aspect} & \textbf{Share Link}
    & \textbf{Download Token} \\
  \midrule
  Usage
    & Reusable (until limit or revoked)
    & One-time use only \\
  Validity
    & Configurable expiry (optional)
    & Fixed 30 minutes \\
  Download Limit
    & Configurable (optional)
    & Exactly 1 download \\
  Revocation
    & Can be revoked at any time
    & Cannot be revoked \\
  Use Case
    & Share with multiple people
    & Send to one specific recipient \\
  Authentication
    & Not required (public)
    & Not required (public) \\
  Watermark
    & Applied (identity: ``Public'')
    & Applied (identity: ``Public'') \\
  \bottomrule
\end{tabularx}

\subsection{Step-by-Step: Creating a Share Link}
\begin{ugSteps}
  \item Open the document detail page.
  \item Navigate to the \ugMenu{Share} tab.
  \item Click \ugButton{Create Share Link}.
  \item Optionally set an \ugField{Expiry Date} and
        \ugField{Max Downloads}.
  \item Click \ugButton{Generate}.
  \item Copy the generated link and share it with recipients.
\end{ugSteps}

\subsection{Step-by-Step: Generating a Download Token}
\begin{ugSteps}
  \item Open the document detail page.
  \item Click \ugButton{Generate Token}.
  \item A one-time download link is generated (valid for
        30~minutes).
  \item Copy and send the link to the intended recipient.
\end{ugSteps}

\subsection{Share Link Validation}

When a recipient accesses a share link, the system performs
the following validation chain:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=18pt]
  \item Is the token valid and active (not revoked)?
  \item Has the link expired (past its expiry date)?
  \item Has the download limit been reached?
  \item Is the linked document still active
        (\texttt{IsActive = true})?
  \item Has the document passed its own expiry date?
\end{enumerate}

If any check fails, access is denied with an appropriate
error message.

\begin{ugWarning}
  Download tokens cannot be revoked once generated. Use
  share links (which can be revoked) for situations where
  you may need to retract access later.
\end{ugWarning}
