\section{AI-Powered Intelligence}

\begin{ugModule}{Overview}

  \textbf{Purpose:}
  Enable intelligent document search and natural language Q\&A
  using a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system.

  \textbf{Who Can Access:}
  All authenticated users.
\end{ugModule}

\subsection{Full-Text Search}

The search bar in the header performs full-text search across:

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=18pt]
  \item Document titles
  \item Tags
  \item Descriptions
  \item \textbf{Extracted document content} --- the actual text
    inside the PDF, extracted at upload time
\end{itemize}

\subsection{AI Chat Assistant}

The AI Chat feature allows you to ask questions in natural
language and receive grounded answers with source citations:

\begin{ugSteps}
  \item Open the \ugMenu{AI Chat} panel.
  \item Type your question in natural language
        (e.g., ``What is the procedure for equipment
        procurement?'').
  \item The system performs hybrid retrieval (dense + sparse
        search) across all ingested documents.
  \item Results are reranked by relevance and passed to a
        language model.
  \item You receive an answer with source citations
        (\texttt{[S1]}, \texttt{[S2]}, etc.) linking to the
        specific document and passage.
\end{ugSteps}

\subsection{How Ingestion Works}

When a document is uploaded, the AI Ingest Worker automatically
processes it:

\begin{enumerate}[leftmargin=18pt]
  \item \textbf{Parse} --- Docling extracts structured content
        (text, tables, figures) from the PDF. For scanned
        documents, OCR (Tesseract) is applied first.
  \item \textbf{Chunk} --- Content is split into semantic
        segments with contextual overlap for continuity.
  \item \textbf{Embed} --- BGE-M3 generates both dense (1024d)
        and sparse vector representations for each chunk.
  \item \textbf{Store} --- Vectors are indexed in the Qdrant
        vector database for fast similarity search.
\end{enumerate}

This process runs \textbf{asynchronously} via Redis Streams
and does not block the upload.

\subsection{RAG Pipeline}

When a user asks a question, the system follows this pipeline:

\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{@{} c l X @{}}
  \toprule
  \textbf{Step} & \textbf{Component}
    & \textbf{Action} \\
  \midrule
  1 & Query Router
    & Gemini classifies the query as document-related
      or general conversation \\
  2 & Hybrid Retriever
    & Dense + sparse search in Qdrant retrieves
      the top-$k$ candidate chunks \\
  3 & Reranker
    & BGE-Reranker-Base cross-encoder reranks candidates
      by semantic relevance \\
  4 & Answer Generator
    & Gemini~2.5~Flash generates a grounded answer using
      the reranked passages as context \\
  5 & Citation Mapper
    & Source references (\texttt{[S1]}, \texttt{[S2]})
      are mapped to specific documents and pages \\
  \bottomrule
\end{tabularx}

\begin{ugTip}
  The AI Chat provides source citations with every answer.
  Always click the source links to verify information
  against the original document. The AI does not fabricate
  information when source passages are available.
\end{ugTip}

\begin{ugWarning}
  AI answers are generated from document content and should
  be verified before making decisions. The system is designed
  to refuse answers when no relevant source material is found.
\end{ugWarning}
